How to Use This Website

  • Registering as a User: Donors can register to create an account, and orphanages can register to showcase their needs.
  • Logging In: Use your credentials to log in to your respective account.
  • Viewing Needs: Browse through the needs listed by orphanages to see how you can help.
  • Making Donations: Follow instructions on the orphanage profiles to donate.
  • Updating Information: Keep your orphanage profile updated with current needs.
  • Getting Help: Click the "Help?" button for assistance or support.
  • Following Us: Connect with us on social media for updates.
  • Feedback and Suggestions: Share your feedback in the "Help" section.
